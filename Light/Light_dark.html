A Light In The Moon

Animation '19

A Light In The Moon is a short 2D animation I created inspired by Gertrude Stein's poem Tender Buttons. I draw it frame by frame in Adobe Photoshop with my Wacom tablet. All the music and sound effects in the video are from the prelude of Soul Searching by X-Ray Dog.
