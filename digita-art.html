Classic of Mountains and Seas / Island

“The Classic of Mountains and Seas” is a Chinese classic text and compilation of mythic geography and myth. It is an ancient writing coming down for more than two thousand years, and it records a great number of mythical monsters in mythologies and folktales. Inspired by this ancient Chinese fairy tale, I created the images of eight different monsters.
