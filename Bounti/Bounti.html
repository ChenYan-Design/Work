Local Bounti

Packaging '19

Fresh, hydroponic, and locally grown. From us to you, in 24 hours.

At Local Bounti, we strive to deliver the freshest, locally grown produce in your neighborhood. We sustainably grow fresh greens and herbs 365 days a year in our northwest greenhouses, using 90% less water and 90% less land than conventional agriculture. The perfect environmental greenhouse conditions ensures the highest possible nutritional value and taste, and we are non-GMO and pesticide / herbicide free. Our sustainable, root-on, living products result in less environmental impacts, carbon footprint, and less waste. It’s our goal to bring our farm to your kitchen.
