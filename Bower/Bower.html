Bowers Market

Branding '20

Bowers Market focus on high margin, differentiated sales. At Bowers Market, our mission is to be a destination for locals and travelers alike by providing fresh and house-made foods, groceries, and unique gifts, all in a clean and welcoming environment.

Bowerbirds are renowned for the elaborate structures the males build and decorate with colourful objects to attract a mate. Just like bowerbirds, Bowers Market bring everyday awesomeness to all customers with it's best collections. For store category signs, we created the illustrations in a traditional etching style to show the uniqueness of Bowers Market.
