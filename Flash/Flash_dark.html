Flash

Zine '18

Flash is a set of zines that allows you to turn your phone's into a mini projector.

Step 1. Fold the back cover into a small box.

Step 2. Attach it on your phone, and turn your flashlight on.

Step 3. Choose the pattern you like and insert it in.

Step 4. Insert the color filter in the zine.
